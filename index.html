Vaccination Information

Vaccination Schedule

Recommended Vaccines by Age

  • Birth: Hepatitis B (1st dose)
  • 1-2 months: Hepatitis B (2nd dose), DTaP, Hib, Polio, PCV, Rotavirus
  • 4 months: DTaP, Hib, Polio, PCV, Rotavirus
  • 6 months: DTaP, Hib, PCV, Rotavirus, Influenza (yearly)
  • 12-15 months: MMR, Varicella, Hib, PCV, Hepatitis A
  • 18 months: DTaP, Hepatitis A
  • 4-6 years: DTaP, MMR, Varicella, Polio

Tips for Vaccinations

  • Keep a record of all vaccinations.
  • Schedule appointments in advance.
  • Comfort your baby during and after vaccinations.
  • Monitor for any side effects and consult your pediatrician if needed.

Family Planning

Postpartum Family Planning

Options for Family Planning

  • Birth Control Pills: Hormonal pills to prevent pregnancy.
  • IUD (Intrauterine Device): Long-term, reversible contraception.
  • Condoms: Barrier method to prevent pregnancy and STIs.
  • Implants: Small rods inserted under the skin for long-term contraception.
  • Natural Methods: Tracking ovulation and fertility awareness.

Tips for Family Planning

  • Discuss options with your healthcare provider.
  • Consider your long-term family goals.
  • Choose a method that fits your lifestyle and health needs.
  • Stay informed about the effectiveness and side effects of each method.

Postpartum Mental Health

Mental Health Resources

Signs of Postpartum Depression

  • Persistent sadness or hopelessness.
  • Loss of interest in activities.
  • Difficulty bonding with your baby.
  • Extreme fatigue or insomnia.
